# 📱 Social Media Etiquette: Do's and Don'ts for Students

As a Student Digital Ambassador, I have developed this checklist to help my peers at VIT Bhopal maintain a professional and safe digital footprint.

### ✅ The Do's (Best Practices)
* **Keep Profiles Professional:** Use a clean, high-quality profile picture, especially on platforms like LinkedIn.
* **Verify Before Sharing:** Always double-check the facts and sources of a post before sharing it to prevent the spread of misinformation.
* **Adjust Privacy Settings:** Regularly review your account privacy settings to control who can see your personal posts and information.
* **Network Meaningfully:** Use social media to connect with industry professionals, alumni, and academic communities in a polite manner.
* **Showcase Your Growth:** Use platforms to share your academic projects, certifications, and learning milestones like DSA progress.

### ❌ The Don'ts (Avoid These)
* **Don’t Post Sensitive Info:** Never share your registration number, home address, phone number, or OTPs on public platforms.
* **Don’t Engage in Cyberbullying:** Avoid negative comments, harassment, or sharing content that could hurt others' reputations.
* **Don’t Over-Share Locations:** Avoid tagging your exact real-time location, which can pose a physical safety risk.
* **Don’t Use Unprofessional Handles:** Avoid using offensive or "cool" usernames for academic or professional networking; stick to your name.
* **Don’t Click Suspicious Links:** Never click on "win a prize" links or unverified ads that could lead to phishing or malware.
